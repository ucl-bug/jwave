    rayleigh_integral

    Rayleigh integral for a given pressure field on a finite plane. See eq. (A.2) in [Sapozhnikov et al.].

    u(r)=12π∫Σu(x)∂n(eik0∣r−x∣∣r−x∣)dΣ(x) u(\mathbf{r}) = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_\Sigma u(\mathbf{x}) \partial_n \left( \frac{e^{ik_0|\mathbf{r} - \mathbf{x}|}}{|\mathbf{r} - \mathbf{x}|} \right) d\Sigma(\mathbf{x}) u(r)=2π1​∫Σ​u(x)∂n​(∣r−x∣eik0​∣r−x∣​)dΣ(x)

    where Σ\SigmaΣ is the domain of integration, corresponding to the finite plane where uuu is defined (the algorithm assumes that the field is identically zero outside such domain), and k0k_0k0​ is the wavenumber of the wave.
