    mass_conservation_rhs

    Implements the mass conservation equation with split fields, see eq. (2.1), (2.8) and (2.17) of the k-Wave manual, which is given by

    ∂∂tuε=−ρ0∂∂εuε+SM \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \mathbf{u_\varepsilon} = - \rho_0 \frac{\partial}{\partial \varepsilon} u_\varepsilon + S_M ∂t∂​uε​=−ρ0​∂ε∂​uε​+SM​

    where uεu_\varepsilonuε​ is the velocity field for the ε\varepsilonε coordinate, ρ0\rho_0ρ0​ is the ambient density, and SMS_MSM​ is the mass source term

    momentum_conservation_rhs

    Implements the momentum conservation equation, see eq. (2.1) of the k-Wave manual, which is given by

    ∂∂tu=−1ρ0∇p \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \mathbf{u} = - \frac{1}{\rho_0} \nabla p ∂t∂​u=−ρ0​1​∇p

    where ρ0\rho_0ρ0​ is the background density, ppp is the pressure, and u\mathbf{u}u is the velocity field. Note that this operator expects both the pressure field and velocity field as input, to ease the implementation of other customized variants that may depend on both fields.

    pressure_from_density

    Evaluates the pressure field from the acoustic density, as

    p=c02∑ερε p = c_0^2 \sum_{\varepsilon} \rho_\varepsilon p=c02​ε∑​ρε​

    where c0c_0c0​ is the speed of sound, ρε\rho_\varepsilonρε​ is the acoustic density, and ε\varepsilonε is the coordinate.

    simulate_wave_propagation

    Simulates wave propagation by integrating the system of equations described in eq. (2.1) of the k-Wave manual, using a first-order symplectic integrator.
